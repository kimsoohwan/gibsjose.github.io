About Me...

I am a computer engineer, outdoorsman, programmer, hockey player, scientist, brewer, classical guitar player, former NASA and CERN researcher, language learner, space nut, and traveller.

I currently live in the great state of Michigan and have previously lived in Washington D.C. and Geneva, Switzerland. I love culture, travel, and learning new languages; I'm fluent in my native English and Spanish, intermediate in French, and a beginner in Russian.

My wife Cruz and I are avid hikers, backpackers, campers ... well, we pretty much love anything outdoors. From sleeping on the beach in Northern Michigan to mountain climbing in Switzerland, it's where we feel at home.
