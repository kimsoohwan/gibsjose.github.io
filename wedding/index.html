Joe & Cruz

Our Story

We met at Grand Valley State University in 2011, and have been dating for more than three years. In August of last year, we decided to take the next step during our annual camping trip at Power Island in TC. We have been together through three years of college in Grand Rapids, and through periods of long distance relationship with Cruz being a beach-bum in Traverse City, and Joe building spaceships in D.C. and climbing mountains in Switzerland.

 

We are excited to finally get married in the beauty of Northern Michigan and have our friends and family be part of this special day!

Joe

Joe is an engineer, scientist, traveller, and brewer. A Grand Rapids native, he loves science, technology, and writing code. He also loves the outdoors, from hiking in Northern Michigan to mountain climbing in Switzerland.

Cruz

Cruz is a Northern Michigan girl, although she is originally from Mexico. She loves to go hiking and camping and enjoy the beauty of Traverse City and the surrounding areas. She studied international relations and Spanish.
